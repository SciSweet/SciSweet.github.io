Key Features

200+ Specialized Tools

Grids and guides, blots cropping and annotations, scale bars, inset, channel split/merge and pseudo-color, significance marks and tables, panel layout with advanced alignment, distribution, and batch export.

Vector-Based Output

Stroke borders, line markers, scale bars, significance marks, tables, and dimension markers are all generated as vector shape layers. Crisp at any resolution, ready for journal submission without rasterization artifacts.

Easy-to-Use

Select your target, set a few options, hit Go. If something isn't right, the plugin tells you exactly what to fix. Click, long-press, right-click, and scroll wheel give you full control without leaving the panel.

17-Language Localization

中文, 日本語, 한국어, العربية, Deutsch, English, Español, Español (Latinoamérica), Français, Indonesia, Italiano, Português (Brasil), Português (Portugal), Русский, Türkçe. Your whole research team covered, worldwide.

Single-Step Undo

In Photoshop, a single plugin action may modify dozens of layers, normally requiring many undo steps to revert. SciSuite wraps each operation into one history state, so one Ctrl+Z takes you right back.

Preset System

Save your settings, export them as files, and share across your lab. Everyone gets the same style, consistent figures, zero setup when switching machines or onboarding new members.
